Welcome to TCG Price Tracker!

Whether you're a veteran Pokemon TCG player, or just enjoy looking at pokemon card art, this website is for you!

Search Pokemon Cards using the sidebar and add them to your favorites to look at them whenever you want!

Click on a card's image to see more information.

Search Criteria

In the search criteria box on the left hand side, you can find a list of parameters to search cards by.

Criteria include Name, Rarity, Type, Subtype, and Supertype

Type refers to the elemental attribute on a pokemon card.

Supertype defines whether you want to look for Pokemon, Trainer, or Energy cards.

Subtypes allow you to search by specific categories of cards (ex. VMAX or GX)

Rarity lets you filter by specific holographic types.

The name field is NOT REQUIRED in searches.

Favorites List

The favorites list allows you to save all your favorite cards in one place!

To add a card to your favorites, click the star underneath their card art.

To view your favorites, click the "Favorites" button in the sidebar.

Favorites are saved to local storage and will always be there whenever you revisit!

Price Conversion

TCG Price Tracker allows you to view card prices in different forms of crypto currency.

Use the dropdown in the top right to view the different currencies prices can be converted into.

Available options include: Bitcoin(BTC), Ethereum(ETH), Binance Coin(BNB), Tether(USDT), Cardano(ADA), Ripple(XRP), and Doge Coin(DOGE).
